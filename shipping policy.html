Welcome to Fusion Wear

Welcome to Fusion Wear, your ultimate destination for premium, customizable T-shirts!

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). Orders placed after 5:00 PM IST or on weekends and public holidays will be processed the next business day.

Shipping Time

We strive to deliver your order as quickly as possible. Typically, orders are shipped and delivered within 5-7 business days from the date of order confirmation, depending on your location.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges vary based on your delivery location and order size. The exact shipping fee will be calculated at the checkout before you complete your purchase. We offer free shipping on orders above a certain value, which will be specified on the checkout page.

Shipping Methods

We partner with reliable courier services to ensure timely delivery. Depending on your location, we may use local and national carriers to deliver your order safely and on time.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email or WhatsApp message with tracking information so you can monitor the progress of your delivery.

Delivery Address

Please ensure that you provide an accurate and complete shipping address. We are not responsible for delays or issues caused by incorrect address details. If an order is returned to us due to an incorrect address, additional shipping fees may apply for reshipment.

International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. We do not offer international shipping at this time.

Damaged or Lost Orders

If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact us within 7 days of receiving the product, and we will assist you in resolving the issue.

Shipping Delays

While we aim to deliver all orders promptly, there may be occasional delays due to factors beyond our control, such as weather conditions, carrier delays, or holidays. In such cases, we will notify you promptly.
